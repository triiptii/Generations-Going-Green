WHAT WE DO?

We, at Generations going green create handmade paper, wrapping paper, notepads, diy bookmarks using waste papers and unused materials. This helps us not waste any usable products and we contribute a fraction to save the nature by reusing these papers. And we sell these products during the college events.
Recycling is very important as waste has a huge negative impact on the natural environment. Harmful chemicals and greenhouse gasses are released from rubbish in landfill sites. Recycling helps to reduce the pollution caused by waste.

ABOUT US

We reuse single sided A4 size papers to make books and notepads. We reuse write ups and journals, waste papers, decorative materials from greeting cards, covers, gift wrapping papers etc to make book marks and cover pages for the books created. Till now we have reused 5000+pages collected from our college campus itself. We intend to donate our profit to PUSH(People United for Students’ Help), an initiative taken up by our college for the students who are financially weak and unable to pay their regular college fees.
